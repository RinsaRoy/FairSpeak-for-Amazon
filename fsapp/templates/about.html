Key Features of FairSpeak

Sentiment Generation

FairSpeak employs advanced natural language processing models to generate sentiment labels for review statements, aiding in understanding the emotional tone of feedback.

Score Prediction

FairSpeak predicts sentiment scores for review statements, providing users with quantitative assessments of feedback sentiment ranging from negative to positive.




Amazon URL Scraping and CSV Conversion

FairSpeak simplifies the process of gathering valuable consumer insights by extracting Amazon product URLs and transforming the associated reviews into structured CSV files, facilitating comprehensive analysis and decision-making.

Product Analysis from CSV

FairSpeak allows users to upload CSV files containing Amazon product reviews for analysis. It predicts the general sentiment, total reviews, and number of positive and negative reviews for the specified product.
